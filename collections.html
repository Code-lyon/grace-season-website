Graceland IV

The Graceland Collection by Grace Season is inspired by the biblical journey of the Israelites from Egypt to the Promised Land, capturing moments of faith, deliverance, and God’s presence. Graceland 1: Represents the beginning of the journey from Egypt (Exodus 12:31–42). Graceland 2: Symbolizes God’s guidance through the pillar of cloud by day and fire by night (Exodus 13:21–22). Graceland 3: Depicts the miraculous parting of the Red Sea by Moses (Exodus 14:21–22). Graceland 4: Reflects the sea closing after the Israelites’ safe passage, securing their deliverance (Exodus 14:26–28). Each piece in the collection tells a story of divine direction, protection, and victory, reminding us of God’s faithfulness on the journey to destiny.

The Ark Collection

ABOUT THE ARK COLLECTION The Ark Collection is inspired by the story of Noah in Genesis 6–9, capturing both the flood and the promise of new beginnings through symbolic artwork. Ark 1 portrays the 40 days and nights of rain and Mount Ararat, where the ark rested after the flood. Ark 2 reflects restoration and covenant, illustrated through the dove, the olive leaf, the rainbow, and Noah’s preservation within the ark. In the New Testament, the ark is revealed as a symbol of salvation through Christ (1 Peter 3:20–21), reminding us that just as Noah and his family were saved through the ark, we are saved through faith in Jesus.
